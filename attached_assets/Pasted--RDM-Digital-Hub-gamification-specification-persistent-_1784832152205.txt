**RDM Digital Hub gamification specification:** persistent **phygital territorial progression system** for Real del Monte, implemented as a stateful, event-driven, season-based game layer over the tourism platform. **Core domain model**
- **Player**: authenticated Supabase user with persistent profile, avatar, rank, season stats, inventory, quest state, and reputation.
- **POI**: geolocated point of interest with type, faction, access rules, reward profile, and mission hooks.
- **Commerce**: merchant entity with category, membership tier, QR identity, validation policy, and reward multipliers.
- **Quest**: narrative or transactional objective with prerequisites, completion conditions, reward bundle, and expiry.
- **Reward**: XP, RDM Points, cosmetic loot, badge, access token, or leaderboard bonus.
- **Season**: 90-day progression window with leaderboard snapshot, decay/reset rules, and festival settlement.
- **Inventory**: persistent collection of cosmetics, badges, skins, titles, and unlock flags.
- **Event**: validated domain event such as check-in, purchase, quest completion, streak bonus, rank-up, or season close.

**Gameplay mechanics**
- **Exploration layer**: users traverse Real del Monte via map-based geofencing and POI discovery.
- **Validation layer**: actions are confirmed by QR scan, geofence proximity, or signed merchant event, with antifraud and idempotency constraints.
- **Progression layer**: each validated action emits XP and RDM Points, updates player state, and advances a nonlinear level curve.
- **Quest layer**: missions are structured as narrative chains, visit sequences, thematic routes, or merchant-linked objectives.
- **Reward layer**: completions grant deterministic and probabilistic outputs, including cosmetics, badges, seasonal bonuses, and access unlocks.
- **Ranking layer**: seasonal leaderboard is computed from weighted XP, mission score, merchant diversity, streaks, and contribution metrics.

**Progression schema**
- **Rank tiers**: `explorer`, `chronicler`, `legendary_miner`, `guardian_of_the_town`.
- **Leveling**: XP-based, monotonic, with increasing marginal cost per level and milestone unlocks.
- **Currency**: RDM Points as soft currency for cosmetic or access unlocks.
- **Streaks**: consecutive validated sessions, check-ins, or purchases amplify reward coefficients.
- **Cosmetics**: avatar skins, titles, auras, map halos, particles, and seasonal insignias.
- **Seasonal logic**: every 90 days the leaderboard is frozen, settlement rewards are emitted, and a new season starts with partial persistence.

**System surfaces**
- **Map view**: POI discovery, quest markers, merchant halos, and geolocation feedback.
- **Profile view**: level, rank, XP, RDM Points, quest completion, inventory, and season stats.
- **Quest hub**: mission catalog, prerequisites, progression states, and reward previews.
- **Leaderboard**: seasonal ranking, score breakdown, and event contributions.
- **Merchant panel**: QR generation, validation status, reward policy, and player throughput.
- **Membership layer**: premium access, bonus multipliers, exclusive quests, and gated rewards.

**Backend event contract**
- `player.registered`
- `poi.discovered`
- `merchant.checked_in`
- `purchase.validated`
- `quest.started`
- `quest.completed`
- `reward.granted`
- `xp.awarded`
- `level.up`
- `rank.promoted`
- `season.closed`
- `leaderboard.snapshot`

**Operational rules**
- All reward issuance must be **server-authoritative**.
- All state transitions must be **idempotent** and **auditable**.
- All merchant interactions must be **signed, validated, and antifraud-checked**.
- All player progression must be **persistent across sessions** and **season-aware**.
- All leaderboard computations must be **deterministic** and **replayable**.

**Design intent**
This is **not** a casual minigame. It is a **territorial tourism RPG-lite with cooperative competition, persistent progression, geospatial interaction, and seasonal reward economics**, built to convert real-world movement and local commerce into structured gameplay.
